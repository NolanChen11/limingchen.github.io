About Me

I'm a programmer, designer and artist.

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Studio Art from Oberlin College in 2023. Now, I'm pursuing a Master's degree in Design Engineering at Brown University and RISD. Through the use of computers as my primary tools, I craft softwares, installations, books, prints, and soundscapes that empower individuals to interpret their surroundings, form connections, and express themselves creatively.

My skills include:

  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Machine Learning (PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-learn)
  • Computer Vision (OpenCV, scikit-image/video, Matplotlib, Tesseract, Pillow, NumPy)
  • Web Development (HTML/CSS/JavaScript, p5.js, ml5.js, some knowledge of Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB)
  • Game Development (Unity3D, C#)
  • Graphic & Interaction Design (Adobe Illustrator/PhotoShop, Arduino, some knowledge of Figma)
  • Other Skills Python, C, C++, Java, Painting, Book Art, Sound Art, Video Editing, Basic 3D modeling in Fusion 360.
